How to wear straight jeans: 7 most versatile looks
Straight jeans are a basic item that can be combined with many. For this review, we have selected the most versatile of them - in different styles and for different situations.
Straight jeans are a classic model that does not leave the list of denim trends from season to season. In addition to the enduring relevance, straight denim trousers are good because the model that emphasizes the dignity of the figure can be chosen for any type of figure. Despite the similarity of the silhouette, straight jeans can be different: long or cropped, with a high, medium or low fit, with different pockets and trims. Even the width of the legs may differ - not to mention the color or dressing of the material.
Another plus of straight jeans is that they can become an indispensable basic thing, suitable for making up a variety of sets. Check out some of the most versatile of them in this review and try them out with your favorite straight leg jeans.
1. Straight jeans + T-shirt
Straight leg jeans and a T-shirt are one of the simplest and most versatile options that can look different. This will depend primarily on the things themselves, that is, jeans and T-shirts - they can be looser or tight, calm or original - and on their environment, that is, shoes and accessories. For example, with sneakers or sneakers and a comfortable backpack, the image will turn out to be relaxed and comfortable, and with model shoes with heels and a small handbag, it will be brighter and more eye-catching. In any case, a T-shirt with straight jeans should be worn tucked in: this way you will create more harmonious proportions of the figure and emphasize the waist.
2. Straight jeans + white shirt


Blue or light blue straight jeans and a white shirt are a modern classic look. It is unlikely that it will become irrelevant in the coming years! This combination, like the previous one, can also be served in different ways. For example, you can match it with calm, elegant flat-heeled shoes, like loafers or men's style boots: you get an elegant and comfortable look, close to smart casual. With sneakers and a scarf around the neck, as in the photo on the left, this set will take on a touch of Parisian style. And if you add it with interesting stilettos and a bright handbag, you can go to a party in this outfit! Like a T-shirt, a shirt with straight-leg jeans looks better tucked in.

If you put on an elegant blouse with straight jeans, then the set immediately takes on a mood that is different from everyday! This look is also perfect for a party, especially if you match it with matching shoes and a handbag. Also, this option will be good for a date, gatherings in a cafe and other similar occasions.
4. Straight jeans + blazer or jacket in men's style

Jeans, worn with a blazer, jacket or jacket in a masculine style, help to create a comfortable and calm, at the same time quite collected look. It is well suited, for example, for an office with a non-strict dress code or for some kind of work meeting. And with comfortable sports shoes, this set will be good for a city walk.
5. Straight jeans + black turtleneck

Another versatile look for all time: a black turtleneck and blue or light blue straight jeans. This set can look different primarily thanks to jeans: for a more relaxed option, choose a classic model, and an interesting pair, for example, from denim with an unusual dressing or some original cut, will help to create an image that attracts attention. In any case, a black turtleneck will give the set an elegant rigor - this effect can be enhanced by choosing the appropriate shoes and bag.




6. Straight jeans + loose fit jumper or sweater

A comfortable set of straight jeans and a loose sweater and pullover will help out all year round - you can dress like this in the autumn-winter season, and in summer, for example, on a cool day. Situations can also be different: jeans with a sweater will be appropriate in a cafe, and on a walk, and in the office, if the rules do not establish strict clothing rules. There are many ways to diversify the kit. Firstly, these are the same shoes and accessories: with sports shoes, this image will look completely different than, for example, with bright shoes or ankle boots of an unusual design. Under the jumper, you can wear a shirt, tucking it in or leaving it loose, a turtleneck or a shirt-front that is now fashionable. Finally, the sweater or jumper itself can be completely or partially tucked into jeans - all this will help create different looks on the basis of one set.

7. Straight jeans + hoodie or sweatshirt

Whether you're going for a walk with your dog, visiting with your child, going to the park with friends, or planning to go shopping in a large shopping mall, this combination will help out in any situation, when you plan to plan an entire activity for this activity. day.
